Some Case Studies & Testimonials

We, hereby, present some case studies to showcase our work. These may not reflect projects executed only at I2R but also past work that clients benefited from, appreciated and endorsed. We, personally take pride in all the work we have done.

Note: Client/brand names have been withheld for confidentiality

Client: Retail Fashion Brand (across GCC)

Market Scenario

Client identified the need to TRACK its brand equity on key brand health parameters across the GCC amid a highly competitive environment. The tracker has been executed regularly since the past 10 years by our team.

Research Objectives

  • To develop a 'single currency' Brand Score to determine the client's brand performance vis-à-vis competitors across markets and monitor initiatives developed through research insights.

Result

Client's marketing ROI improved significantly, with certain initiatives delivering up to 10X returns. Improved key KPIs such as TOM, NPS, influencer and celebrity impact, loyalty program, and overall customer satisfaction—across both tangible and intangible brand touchpoints.

Client: Global Healthcare Company

Market Scenario

Client offers highly reputed glucometer brands. Intended launching a new brand in the MENA markets. Client had identified $'X' as unit price to align with market dynamics.

Research Objectives

  • To determine the optimal product features for differentiation and pricing to gain market share.
  • To understand the role of pharmacists in influencing glucometer brand choice.

Result

The 'optimal price' recommended by the research (an 18% increment over client proposition), was implemented and led to increase in profits and market share across all three markets. Insights also helped design a successful push strategy amongst pharmacists.

Client: Financial Planning Company in the UAE

Market Scenario

Company offers products and services to encourage financial planning. The client identified the need to build an 'Index' that would help understand people's propensity to save in the GCC.

Research Objectives

  • To build and track an 'Index' (a leading indicator) to measure of the propensity to 'save' and determine the 'present and future financial status' of the target audience.

Result

The 'Index' helped the client develop innovative savings instruments catering to different ethnic and income groups and corporations. The client's customer base increased by 3X over a short span as it reinforced a savings culture through tailored products. The Index became a 'Point of Reference' for financial institutions in the region.

Client: Global CD Agency / Global Conglomerate in KSA

Market Scenario

The client had a vision to build a center that would encompass human and cultural themes, provide an avenue for art and creativity, education and entertainment, nurture and expose people's talents from within the Kingdom and beyond.

Research Objectives

  • To explore potential ideas and themes for the design of a center that would inspire minds, encourage creativity and lead to talent discovery.

Result

The 'grand museum' inside Saudi Arabia is testimony to the client's vision. The research was instrumental in guiding the client and its communications partner in identifying 'themes' that would resonate with the people and help design a center that in the client's own words, 'is a window to world experiences, evolving, transformative and innovative'.

Client: Leading Bank in the UAE

Market Scenario

Client identified the need to conduct C-Sat study amongst different B2B and B2C customer segments based on the products/services usage. The study conducted amongst 'competitor' customers as well.

Research Objectives

  • To assess customer experience (CX) with banks.
  • To derive insights to identify services that drive customer loyalty and advocacy (NPS) benchmarked against competitors.

Result

The client was able to identify its CX strengths and weaknesses vis-à-vis competition. Of significance were the findings identifying the reasons for churn amongst its customers of certain ethnicity and in specific geographies.
